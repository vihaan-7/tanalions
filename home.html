Tana Lions

Tana Lions is a cricket team in Madagascar.This team was the "CHAMPIONS OF 2024 CRICKET TOURNAMENT".

Indian Community Cricket Tournament 2024

This tournament was hosted and sponsored by Blueberry Travel Madagascar under the umbrella of Indian Embassy of Antananarivo.There were total of 4 teams :" Tana Lions, Tana Warrior, Tana Titans, Blueberry Tigers ".This tournament was a 8 overs tournament with no additional rules but it followed all the written rules of offical cricket.The final match took place in Ambohitrimanjaka and was attended by Indian Ambassador and Minsters from the Sports Ministry.The teams that faced each other in the final was Tana Lions vs Tana Warriors.
