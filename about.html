About Tana Lions



Tana Lions was created in 2024 for the Indian Cricket Tournament.Our team was just heroic and enthusiastic through out the tournament.We had fast bowling lineup with amazing support by our batting lineup also our fielding players were full with reflexes and pace to save runs and get wickets.Tana Lions's Coach Anurag Rathore, Captain Parwinder Kumar, and Vice Captain Sarfaraz Khan's tactics and how they led the team was legendary. Our team players handled the pressure with such composure, showing their mental strength.The team played 4 matches,won 3 of them and lost 1 match. The final match took place at Ambohitrimanjaka on 22 September,2024.After the victory,we can see how good the team's unity is to accomplish the objective.M.Abbas as the Man of The Match was a well deserved award.Our team is also preparing for next year tournaments every Sunday with other teams.
